Juinor Golf Camp


Hickory Hills PGA Pro John Olson teaches junior golfers the etiquette of the game. Then John will teach the children the basic grip and swing techniques. During the 7 week program your child will grow in the game of golf and manners. The first 4 weeks John will work with them to be able to hit from the tee, fairway, chip and putt. The final 3 weeks the children will enjoy playing the actual game as they get the chance to play the holes at Hickory Hills. Every child wants a shot at number four and making it over the water!

Personal Lessons


Take your game to the next level. Learn how to correct that slice, chip with confidence and read the green to make that long birdie putt. John will help you correct the mistakes your making and improve your game. John can taylor your sessions to the area of your game that is in need of improvement. Lessons can be as little as one session or improve your entire game with a lesson package. Contact the pro shop for more details.

Group Lessons


Get your partner or family together and learn the game of golf. Golf is a great game to be enjoyed with the whole family. Bring them out for some lessons and John will get all of your games going in the right direction. John will take an individual approach and taylor his teaching to each one of you individualy as well as a group. Call the pro shop for more details.

Play with a Pro


Play a round with Hickory Hills PGA Pro John Olson. John will guide you through the course and help you with your shot selction. Playing with a pro can give you great insight on the thinking that goes into every shot and how to position yourself for the lowest possible score. Call the Pro Shop today for more information on how you can play with a pro.
