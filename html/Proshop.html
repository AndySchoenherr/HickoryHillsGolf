The Pro Shop at Hickory Hills Golf Club

The pro shop at Hickory Hills has all your golfing needs. No matter if you need a new golve, a sleeve or box of balls, a stylish new hat, a collectors divot tool, or are looking to pick up a new club, the pro shop has it all. Also at the pro shop you can register for upcoming events, purchase a membership or sign up for a lessons package.


Need a snack or drink to take with you on your round? How about a hot dog at the turn? The snack bar in the pro shop has all your favorites and will fill you up with food and beverage.


You can contact the pro shop by visiting our contact page or calling 812-358-4529
